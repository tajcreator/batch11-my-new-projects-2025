Today's Bangladesh

The Appellate Division has upheld the High Court's decision to dismiss five labour-law related cases against Chief Adviser Prof Muhammad Yunus. A three-judge bench of the Appellate Division, led by Justice Md Ashfaqul Islam, issued the ruling, rejecting the state's appeal for permission to challenge the High Court's verdict. During the court proceedings, Additional Attorney General Mohammad Anik Rushd Haque represented the state, while Mustafizur Rahman Khan represented Yunus.
